Welcome to CAN YOU HEAR US. Each of the red stripes contains a live feed from an American political talk radio station. The stations are randomly generated from a list that includes a diversity of political opinion from all accross the country. Click a stripe to mute that station.

In a media environment that feeds you news you want to hear from people who share your beliefs, I hope this site can help broaden perspectives, even if that means taking a few minutes to listen to somebody you hate.
